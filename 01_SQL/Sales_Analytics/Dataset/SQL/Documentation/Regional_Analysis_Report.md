-- city performance --

-- Loss Making State
Observation: Some states recorded negative profit despite generating sales.
Evidence: The analysis identified states with negative profit (loss-making states) in the dataset.
Business Impact: These states reduce overall business profitability and indicate issues such as high discounts, high costs, or low margins.
Recommendation: Review pricing, discount policies, shipping costs, and product mix in these states, and implement targeted strategies to improve profitability.

-- Regional category performance --
Observation: In the Central region, the Technology category recorded the highest sales and profit.
Evidence: Technology generated the highest sales and profit compared to Office Supplies and Furniture in the Central region.
Business Impact: Technology is the primary driver of revenue and profitability in the Central region.
Recommendation: Continue promoting Technology products in the Central region, maintain adequate inventory, and apply similar sales strategies to improve the performance of Office Supplies and Furniture.

-- Average order value by region -- 
Observation: East region recorded the highest average order value among all regions.
Evidence: The East region has the highest average sales per order compared to the other regions.
Business Impact: Customers in the East region spend more per transaction, contributing to higher revenue per order.
Recommendation: Identify the factors driving higher order values in the East region and replicate those strategies in other regions through product bundling, upselling, and targeted promotions.
